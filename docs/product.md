# Sarap.ai Product Spec

## ICP

Restaurant groups in Almaty and Astana:

- 2-10 branches.
- Google rating around 3.8-4.4.
- 100+ reviews.
- Owner, manager, administrator, or marketer owns review response.

## MVP Promise

Every week Sarap.ai shows which branch is losing reputation, why guests complain, and what the team should reply or fix.

## MVP Features

- User authentication.
- Organization and branch management.
- CSV review import.
- AI review classification: sentiment, category, urgency, language, summary.
- AI reply drafts in Russian and Kazakh.
- Review dashboard filters.
- Branch risk levels.
- Today dashboard.
- Weekly reputation reports.

## Non-Goals For First 90 Days

- Mobile app.
- 2GIS, Kaspi, POS, or iiko integrations.
- Auto-posting replies.
- Competitor intelligence.
- Advanced billing.
- Agency white-label.
- Enterprise permission model.

## Pricing Test

- 14-day pilot.
- 49k KZT/month for 1-2 branches.
- 89k KZT/month for 3-5 branches.
- 149k KZT/month for 6-10 branches.
- Discount only in exchange for a written case study, public testimonial, or warm introductions.
