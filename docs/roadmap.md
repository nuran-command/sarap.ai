# Sarap.ai 90-Day Launch Roadmap

## Goal

Prove within 90 days that restaurant groups in Almaty and Astana will pay for an AI reputation management system that turns reviews into urgent actions, response drafts, branch risk, and weekly reports.

## Days 1-15: Customer Discovery

- Build a list of 50 restaurant groups in Almaty and Astana.
- Run 15 founder-led interviews with owners, managers, administrators, or marketers.
- Validate four pains: slow review response, rating drops, repeated complaints, no weekly review process.
- Ask for CSV/manual review exports from at least 5 prospects.

Success criteria:

- 8+ prospects confirm the pain is active.
- 5+ prospects agree to share review data.
- 3+ prospects agree to test a weekly report.

## Days 16-30: Concierge MVP

- Manually import review CSVs for 3-5 restaurants.
- Send a weekly report with top complaints, worst branch, urgent reviews, suggested replies, and recommended actions.
- Record what managers use, ignore, or ask to change.

Success criteria:

- 3+ restaurants ask for another weekly report.
- 80% of suggested replies need only minor edits.
- Each report produces at least one manager-owned action.

## Days 31-60: Product MVP

- Build the dashboard flow: organization -> branches -> CSV import -> analysis -> replies -> weekly report.
- Keep the AI quality checklist strict: no compensation promises, no customer blame, under 90 words, professional tone.
- Make onboarding self-serve enough for pilots to upload CSV without manual formatting help.

Success criteria:

- 5 pilot restaurants can upload CSVs and get a usable report.
- Time-to-first-report is under 15 minutes after receiving a CSV.
- Negative and urgent reviews are easy to filter.

## Days 61-90: Paid Pilot

- Convert pilots into paid customers.
- Track weekly active usage, accepted AI replies, urgent review response rate, and repeat CSV uploads.
- Collect one public or anonymous case study.

Success criteria:

- 3 paying customers.
- At least 2 customers use Sarap.ai for 2 consecutive weeks.
- Day 90 decision: bootstrap, accelerator/grant, or pre-seed only if paid traction exists.
